My Journey

From academic studies to award-winning startup development - here's my story

The Beginning

While pursuing my Bachelor's Honours degree in Political Science at Dhaka College from 2009-2013, I discovered my passion for technology and software development. What started as curiosity about how websites worked quickly became a fascination with creating digital solutions that could solve real-world problems.

Balancing my academic studies with technology training, I took the IELTS exam in 2010 and scored 6.5 in the academic module, which strengthened my foundation in English - an essential skill for technical documentation and global collaboration. I then pursued computer basics training at NIIT and specialized web design and development courses, all while continuing my formal education.

"The best way to learn is by doing. Every project, no matter how small, taught me something new."

Education & Foundation

I completed my Bachelor's Honours degree in Political Science from 2009-2013 at Dhaka College, followed by my Master's degree in the same subject at Dhaka College the following year. While pursuing my formal academic education, I simultaneously engaged in technology-related studies and skill development, laying a strong foundation for my future career in software development.

During my academic years, I balanced my political science studies with intensive technology training, including the IELTS exam, computer basics at NIIT, and specialized web design and development courses. This dual focus allowed me to develop both analytical thinking through my academic studies and technical expertise through focused technology training.

Professional Growth

Upon completing my Master's degree in 2014, I dove into the professional world with my first job at Phase 3 Solutions in 2013, where I worked until February 2016. This role provided me with invaluable real-world experience in building and maintaining web applications for clients across various industries. I learned the importance of writing maintainable, scalable code and developed strong problem-solving skills in a collaborative environment.

After leaving Phase 3 Solutions in February 2016, I transitioned to full-time freelancing on Upwork (formerly Odesk), where I have been working primarily ever since. This path allowed me to work with diverse clients globally and tackle a wide variety of technical challenges while maintaining the flexibility to continue my education and personal development.

Leadership & Mentorship

From 2022 to 2024, I had the opportunity to work with a German company, MentalStark, where I served as a core developer from the beginning to the completion of their Symfony framework-based project. This project utilized API Platform and Domain-Driven Design (DDD) methodologies. My role involved not only technical development but also mentoring junior developers and contributing to architectural decisions.

The project was highly successful and received recognition as the "Most Promising Startup in Germany" in 2024. This experience reinforced my belief in the importance of strong technical leadership, clear communication, and understanding business requirements to deliver exceptional software solutions.

Embracing New Technologies

After my successful collaboration with MentalStark, I returned to full-time freelancing on Upwork, where I continue to work with clients globally. My experience with modern frameworks like Symfony, API Platform, and DDD methodologies has enhanced my ability to build robust, scalable applications.

I stay current with evolving technologies and methodologies, ensuring my solutions are cutting-edge and aligned with industry best practices. My focus remains on delivering high-quality, maintainable code while leveraging the latest tools and frameworks.
